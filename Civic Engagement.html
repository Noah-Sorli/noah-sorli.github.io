Civic Engagement

As future engineers, I believe it is essential to remain aware of the world around us and of the societal and environmental challenges that shape it. Technical skills alone are not enough. Understanding human needs and acting responsibly are key to creating meaningful and sustainable solutions. Through different projects and initiatives, I aim to contribute, in my own way, to building a more conscious and balanced world.

Capitole du libre

The Capitole du Libre is a series of conferences hosted at ENSEEIHT, dedicated to open-source software and digital freedom. This year, I will take part by assisting through the school’s technical associations, Photo7 and TVn7. Even though I am not in the computer science department, I strongly believe in the value and philosophy of open-source software, which promotes collaboration, accessibility, and shared innovation.

Solidary trip in Peru in project

This project is currently being organized with the help of Human7, the humanitarian association of ENSEEIHT. Our goal is to travel to Peru for several weeks to support local communities, mainly by assisting young people through educational activities and small construction projects. This experience would be a meaningful opportunity to combine teamwork, cultural exchange, and social impact.
